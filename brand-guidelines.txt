# AHMED DIGITAL - BRAND GUIDELINES

## 1. BRAND OVERVIEW & IDENTITY
Ahmed Digital is a premium video editing agency dedicated to helping businesses and creators share their message through high-impact, scroll-stopping content. Our focus is on storytelling that resonates and results that go viral.

**Brand Name:** Ahmed Digital
**Brand Tagline:** "We create stories that you want to tell."
**Core Mission:** To make video editing simple and stress-free for businesses by handling the creative side with care and consistency. We aim to help clients share their message clearly through clean, effective, and well-edited content.

## 5. VISUAL IDENTITY

### A. Color Palette
Our palette is a blend of sophisticated deep purples balanced by high-contrast whites and a vibrant "viral" highlight.

*   **Primary Dark:** #200f48 (Deep Space Purple) - Use for depth and authority.
*   **Secondary:** #391985 (Royal Purple) - Use for UI elements and branding.
*   **Tertiary/Vibrant:** #7e56dc (Electric Lavender) - Use for primary buttons, links, and major accents.
*   **Highlight:** #cfeb6c (Viral Lime) - Use sparingly for high-attention calls-to-action or status indicators.
*   **Backgrounds:** 
    *   Dark: #0a0a0a (Deep Black)
    *   Light: #ffffff (Pure White)
*   **Foregrounds:** #ededed (Off-white) / #171717 (Dark Gray)

### B. Typography
*   **Primary Font:** **Poppins** (Sans-Serif)
    *   Weights: 100 (Thin), 200 (Extra-Light), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold).
    *   Usage: All headings, body text, and interfaces.
*   **Accent Style:** Serif Italic (e.g., for "Go Viral" callouts).
    *   Usage: To emphasize key emotional words or specific brand slogans within headings.
